# TeraMotors - Feature #10: CRM & Marketing Automation
**Implementation Plan**

## Executive Summary
**Goal:** Automated email/SMS campaigns for customer retention and revenue growth

**Impact:** 35% increase in repeat customers, 4x ROI on marketing spend

**Timeline:** 8-10 weeks | **Complexity:** High

## Features

### Customer Segmentation
- New customers (welcome series)
- Lapsed customers (win-back campaign)
- High-value customers (VIP treatment)
- Overdue service (maintenance reminders)

### Automated Campaigns
1. **Welcome Series** (new customer onboarding)
2. **Appointment Reminders** (reduce no-shows)
3. **Service Reminders** (based on mileage/time)
4. **Birthday Offers** (loyalty building)
5. **Review Requests** (post-service)
6. **Win-Back** (inactive customers)

### Marketing Tools
- Email builder (drag-drop templates)
- SMS campaigns (bulk messaging)
- A/B testing
- Analytics dashboard (open rates, CTR, conversions)
- Customer lifetime value (CLV) tracking

### Integration
- Mailchimp / SendGrid for emails
- Twilio for SMS
- Pre-built templates
- Personalization variables

**End of Feature #10 Plan**
